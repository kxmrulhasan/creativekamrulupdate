FAQ

Common questions

What services do you offer? +
I offer brand identity design, UX/UI design, design system creation, and product strategy consulting. I work with startups, agencies, and established companies.
How long does a typical project take? +
Project timelines vary by scope. A brand identity project typically takes 3–6 weeks, while a full product design engagement may run 2–4 months. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our discovery call.
Do you work with international clients? +
Absolutely! I work with clients globally. Most of my collaboration happens asynchronously, with scheduled video calls to align on milestones and decisions.
What's your pricing structure? +
I work on both fixed-price projects and monthly retainers. Pricing depends on scope and complexity. Reach out with your project details and I'll provide a custom quote within 24 hours.
