Know who am I

My Professional Journey

I craft intuitive, user-centered experiences across web and mobile platforms, transforming complex ideas into elegant and functional digital solutions. Having collaborated with both small agencies and mid-sized companies, I bring a versatile design approach that adapts to diverse business needs and user contexts.

My experience spans eLearning, eCommerce, service platforms, and financial applications, where I focus on usability, accessibility, and scalable design systems. I specialize in creating seamless user journeys, modern interfaces, and consistent multi-device experiences using industry-standard tools such as Figma.

Currently, I am dedicated to designing impactful digital products that enhance user engagement, improve accessibility, and deliver meaningful value to users and organizations alike.

👋 Let's talk * Response time is typically around 12 hours
Are you available to hire full time? ⌄
At the moment, I'm pretty happy where I am. Currently I am not looking for any full-time opportunities. But that being said, I am always open to discuss interesting opportunities, collaborations and other fun stuff. If you're interested in discussing a project, making something great together, please fill the form above. Simply want to get in touch? Happy to connect on socials.
How do your quote pricing works and when can we get on call? ⌄
Pricing varies by project scope and complexity. I offer both fixed-price and retainer engagements. After a brief discovery call (typically 30 min), I'll send a detailed proposal within 24 hours. You can book a slot directly through my calendar link.
Can you facelift my design? ⌄
Absolutely! I offer design audits and UI facelifts for existing products. This includes a full review of your current design, identifying pain points, and delivering a refreshed interface that aligns with your brand and user needs.
What tools and deliverables do you work with? ⌄
I primarily work in Figma for design and prototyping, along with FigJam for workshops. Deliverables include design files, component libraries, design tokens, and handoff-ready specs — whatever your team needs to build confidently.
Do you work with international clients? ⌄
Yes! I work with clients globally. Most collaboration happens asynchronously, with scheduled video calls to align on key milestones. Timezone differences haven't been a blocker for any of my past projects.
