How I Work

My Design
Process

01
🔍
STEP 01
Discover & Research
I start by deeply understanding the problem space — talking to users, studying competitors, and mapping business goals to uncover real insights.
User Interviews Competitor Analysis Empathy Maps
02
💡
STEP 02
Define & Ideate
Turning raw research into clear problem statements, then rapidly generating ideas through sketching, brainstorming, and collaborative workshops.
How Might We User Stories Sketching
03
📐
STEP 03
Wireframe & Structure
Building low-fidelity wireframes to explore layout, hierarchy, and flows — fast and iterative before committing to visual details.
Lo-fi Wireframes Information Architecture
04
🎨
STEP 04
Design & Prototype
Crafting high-fidelity UI with meticulous attention to typography, spacing, and interactions — then building clickable prototypes to bring it to life.
Hi-fi Design Design System Figma
05
🧪
STEP 05
Test & Validate
Running usability tests with real users, collecting feedback, and iterating rapidly until the design feels intuitive and frictionless.
Usability Testing A/B Testing Heatmaps
06
🚀
STEP 06
Deliver & Iterate
Handing off polished assets with developer specs, then monitoring post-launch metrics and continuously improving based on real-world data.
Dev Handoff Zeplin / Inspect Analytics
